Hi! Welcome to PrinterConnect. We are a small group of University students who want to gather information for our product!

What are we about?

We are developing a solution to make printer management easier for small hospitality businesses. We plan on creating a device which connects to many different printers regardless of their brand or model, and links to a mobile app which provides a simple interface for managing orders.

We would love if you could help us by completing a small google form which will help us improve our product!
